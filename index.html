Quest Till Done

A unified approach to software development

QTD attempts to simplify code maintenance and competing priorities, providing:

A high level overview of all projects, their progress, and approaching deadlines

An at a view glance of a project's progress, the relationship between issues, and current goals.

And automated prioritization to allow programmers to focus on getting the work done rather than wading through a pile of issues
